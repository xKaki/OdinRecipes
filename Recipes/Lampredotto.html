Lampredotto Recipe

Description

Panini di lampredotto is a sandwich that consists of tripe, made from cow’s fourth stomach cooked in a variety of ways but usually topped with salsa verde. The trip consists of two separate layers of meat; one lean, dark and flavorful, and one pale, fatty, and relatively blunt. The meat is prepared in a broth similarly like the shkembe chorba (tripe soup) we previously featured on our website. Once ready, the meat is added in a sandwich and topped with some delicious salsa verde (and yes, we’ll show you how to do that too).

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Tripe
  • 3 Litres Water
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1 Large Tomato
  • 1 Carrot
  • 5 Black Peppercorns
  • 1 Stalk Celery
  • 2 Tablespoon Salt

steps

  1. Chop the vegetables and add them to the water
  2. Season the broth with salt and peppercorns and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Add the lampredotto and cook it covered with a lid until it becomes soft. This should take between 40 and 60 minutes.
  4. Make sure the lampredotto is submerged under the stew all the time.
  5. Keep the lampredotto warm and in the broth until it becomes time to use it.
